Introduction

Welcome to our comprehensive dashboard that delves into the fascinating world of video games. Using data sourced from the RAWG API, this webpage aims to provide an in-depth analysis of various aspects of the gaming industry. From general trends to financial insights, we've got it all covered. The data has been meticulously processed and visualized to offer you a seamless experience as you explore the gaming universe.
In the first graph of this page, Game Releases Over Time you will see a comprehensive look at the timeline of game releases, offering a historical perspective on the gaming industry's growth and evolution. By examining the number of games released each year, we can identify key periods of innovation, saturation, and transformation.

This section serves as your gateway to understanding the broad landscape of the gaming world. Here, you'll find graphs that break down game releases by platform, genre, and ESRB ratings. These charts offer a snapshot of the industry's diversity and can help you grasp the scale and variety of games available in the market.

Quality and Ratings

Here we focus on metrics that gauge the quality of games, such as Metacritic scores and user ratings. This section aims to provide a nuanced understanding of how games are received critically and popularly. Whether you're a developer looking for benchmarks or a gamer curious about how titles stack up, this section has something for you.

Trends and Popularity

Here we dive yon the interests trends on specific games and genres over time. From the rise and fall of genres over time to which games are most owned and completed, this section offers a dynamic look at the ever-changing tastes and behaviors of gamers.

Financial Aspects

Last but not least, the "Financial Aspects" section delves into the monetary side of the gaming industry. Here, you'll find data on prize money by country, presented both in tabular and graphical formats. Whether you're an investor, a competitor, or just curious, this section provides valuable insights into the financial incentives and rewards in the gaming world.
